Tina & Helen Present

Equestrian professionals, Tina Canton and Helen Bell have worked together and are proud to present their innovative modern take on the riding club.

What is i-Horse?

i-Horse club aims to create a place for all things equine. We will provide a platform where you can do all your horsey online stuff in one place, from buying or selling to coaching tips and advice. We will also provide apps and tools to make your horsey life easier

Joining iHorse is joining our community. We encourage members to interact with each other and want i-Horse to become the one stop shop for all things horsey. The club will launch in April so watch this space, we have big plans for you if you are an equine trader, professional rider, grass roots rider or happy hacker you will not wnat to be without i-Horse

Riding and Tech

Modern day riders can access a whole host of online resources. Technology is becoming another tool in a riders toolbox. Instead of going to different apps, websites or magazines, i-Horse will be the name you can trust to meet all your needs
