Horse Arena Near Heckington

Windward paddocks horse arena hire is near Heckington in Lincolnshire. We are based out on the fen and can be accessed easily from the A17,  SouthKyme , Boston and are near to most locations in the Sleaford area. The surrounding area is nice and quiet and there is a short circular hack by road that can be used as a nice warm up or warm down. Outdoor arena hire can be hit or miss in frosty conditions but our arena being rubber does not easily freeze. We do not have an indoor arena and all being compeition riders we need a service that we can use ourselves all year round.
