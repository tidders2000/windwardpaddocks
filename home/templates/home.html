Welcome to Windward Paddocks Equestrian Centre


Welcome to the Windward Padodcks Equestrian Centre. We are a small family run business in Heckington. 

We took over Windward Paddocks in early 2022 with the vision to restore it to a quality equestrian centre for local riders. We are keen to provide a small friendly yard providingan enjoyable and safe place for riders and horses alike.

We have been involved with horses  for many years and that passion has paased to our children. The four of us are all riders competing in jumping and dressage.
